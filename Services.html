Classes



Beginners

A new cycle of our Baking Classes starts every other Saturday at our #123 San Fernando Street location in San Fernando. The classes are held for the duration of two weekends from 8AM to 4PM, and cost $1,000.00 per participant. All materials are provided and certificates are awarded upon successful completion. By the end of this class, successful participants will be able to:

  • Prepare many delicious types of cakes, pastries and different types of breads
  • Recall the basic science of baking
  • Approximate the correct oven temperature needed to bake various types of bakery items
  • Learn beginner cake & cookie decorating skills
  • Make pie fillings from scratch and much more


Advanced

Our Advanced Class is for participants who already have basic or intermediate knowledge of baking. This 2-week, 32-hour class begins on the first Monday of every month, and is held Mondays-Wednesdays & Fridays from 5PM-9PM. In this class you'll learn how to:

  • Artfully present your baked goods
  • Decorate your creations using advanced skills
  • Apply advanced techniques & tricks
  • Improve upon all other areas covered in the Beginner Class

A deposit of $250.00 is required to secure your spot in our classes. Click here to access the form to sign up.
